Rajat Joshi

Welcome

Hello! I’m Rajat Joshi, a Ph.D. candidate in Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences at Princeton University, advised by Dr. Rong Zhang.

My research interest is broadly in climate dynamics. I have worked on projects related to the impacts of the weakening of AMOC on remote climate, coupled ocean-atmosphere interactions, tropical dynamics and Indian Monsoon Rainfall, and extreme weather events (hurricanes and storm tracks). I am always open to and interested in working on exciting problems beyond my current research interest.

Research
